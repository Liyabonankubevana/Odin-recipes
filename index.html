Burger el King

The Burger is a tradional burger that has the best taste in town with the best ingredients and taste that has stood time!

Trust us, it's delicious

Ingredients

  • Burger Roll
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe Slices
  • Onion slices
  • Burger Patty
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop two slices of red Onion
  2. Chop two slices of Tomatoe
  3. Take a leaf of lettuce
  4. Fry your burger patty for 4-5 mins ensuring your don't over cook it
  5. Once done, assemble the burger
