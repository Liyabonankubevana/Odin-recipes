Umphokoqho

Umphokoqho is a tradional isiXhosa African salad dish that is served with sour milk

A new experience, a better taste

Ingredients

  • Maize meal
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Sour Milk (Amasi)
  1. Pour two cups boiling waters into a pot
  2. Add a teaspoon of salt
  3. Let it cook until salt has evaporated and water is boiling
  4. Then, add two and a half cups of Maize meal
  5. At this point it is all about your hand. You will have to stir the maize meal until you have a fluffy consistency and add 1/2 a cup maize meal and close the lid
  6. let it cook for 10 to 15 mins and keep checking for the right consistency
  7. Once done, serve room temperature with Sour Milk (Amasi)
